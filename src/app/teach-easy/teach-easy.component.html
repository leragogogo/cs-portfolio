Description

The team of TeachEasy startup came to my university to hold a hackathon. The goal was to create a demo version of the application to help Austrian teachers to digitalize their work. We developed a design using Figma, created a database structure, and implemented key functionality of the app using Flutter. My team got the second place out of 13 contesters.
