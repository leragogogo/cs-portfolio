Description

It was a final task in the Flutter course at my University, collaborating with Surf Company. The application provides users with cafes, restaurants, museums, hotels, and other places nearby. A user can filter places by distance and type. The places can be displayed as a list or as points on the map. Dark mode is also available! Last but not least, a user can add places that he or she likes to the favorites screen so as not to forget or to share with friends.
